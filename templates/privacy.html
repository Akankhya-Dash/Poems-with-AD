Legal & Ethics

Privacy Policy

"Your words are the architecture of your soul. We are merely the stewards of the space in which they reside."

Privacy as a Sanctuary

At Poems With AD, we believe that the act of writing poetry is an intimate communion. For this reason, we treat your data not as a commodity to be traded, but as a sacred trust. Our platform is designed to be a digital monastery—a place where silence and security are the foundations of creativity.

When you enter Poems With AD, you are entering a space where surveillance is absent. We do not track your movements across the wider web, nor do we build predatory profiles based on the metaphors you choose or the rhythms of your verse.

"The secret of the poet is hidden in the silence between the lines."

The Information We Gather

To facilitate your journey through Poems With AD, we collect only the most essential elements:

  • account_circle Identity: Your chosen pseudonym and a valid electronic mail address for account recovery.
  • history_edu Manifestations: The poems, drafts, and collections you commit to our digital ink.
  • settings_input_component Technical Echoes: Basic diagnostic data to ensure the platform remains functional on your chosen device.

The Stewardship of Your Words

We do not own your poetry. You grant Poems With AD a limited license to display your work according to your chosen visibility settings (Private, Shared, or Public). We will never sell your words to third-party advertisers or use them to train large-scale language models without your explicit, separate consent.

Your drafts are encrypted at rest, ensuring that the half-finished thoughts and raw emotions of your creative process remain for your eyes only.

The Right to be Forgotten

In the tradition of the erasable manuscript, you maintain absolute control over your legacy. At any moment, you may choose to retract your poems from the public eye or dissolve your account entirely.

Upon request for deletion, we will scrub our servers of your presence within thirty lunar cycles. What has been written may be unwritten, and your digital footprint on Poems With AD will vanish into the stars.

Questions of the Soul?
